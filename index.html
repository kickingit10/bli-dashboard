📊 What Is This Dashboard?

This dashboard transforms your weekly FedEx Ground charge statements into actionable insights. Instead of manually reviewing PDF statements, you get automated tracking of revenue, stop counts, driver performance, contract rates, and surcharges — all in one place.

Data is updated weekly as new charge statements are released by FedEx. The dashboard currently tracks 19 weeks of data from October 2025 through February 2026.

👁 Four Views

The dashboard has four views designed for different needs:

Executive View

High-level financial and operational summary. See weekly settlement, cost trends, contract rate changes, and surcharge impacts at a glance.

Operations View

Driver-by-driver performance breakdown. Workload scoring, package mix, and efficiency across your team. Best for route optimization and coaching.

Trends View

Period-over-period comparisons. Peak vs non-peak, monthly trends, and contract period analysis. Best for strategic planning.

Daily View

Day-of-week analysis showing volume patterns across Mon-Sat. Staffing insights, busiest vs lightest days, and e-commerce trends. Best for reroute and staffing decisions.

📈 Key Metrics Explained

Net Revenue
Total settlement after all charges, deductions, and adjustments
Net / Route
Net settlement per driver-day — what each route earns you after all charges
LP %
Large Package rate (formerly DESIG). Changes with new contract periods (7.63% → 7.19% → 7.07%)
Pkg / Stop
Average packages per stop — higher means more efficient dense routes
Stops / Driver-Day
How many stops each driver completes per working day
Peak / Surcharges
Seasonal charges: Surge Stop ($13.50/stop), Peak Service ($1,100/wk), P&D adjustments

Hover for Details

Hover over any KPI card at the top of the dashboard to see a detailed tooltip breakdown. The Net/Route card shows per-contract-period averages.

📅 Date Filtering

Use the date range picker in the top-right corner to focus on specific time periods:

  • 1
    Preset Ranges: Quick buttons for Last 4 Weeks, Last 8 Weeks, Last 13 Weeks (quarter), Year-to-Date, and All Data.
  • 2
    Custom Range: Select "Custom" from the dropdown, then pick specific start and end dates to zoom into any period.
  • 3
    Contract Awareness: The dashboard automatically shows color-coded bands on charts indicating which contract period applies (blue, green, or gold).

👥 Driver Tags

In the Operations view, drivers are automatically tagged based on their work patterns:

  • B
    Bulk — Primarily handles large/bulk deliveries with high package counts but fewer stops
  • M
    Manager — Operations manager who works limited days but handles dispatch/pickup-heavy roles
  • N
    New / Ramp — Driver with fewer than 3 working days, likely new or part-time
